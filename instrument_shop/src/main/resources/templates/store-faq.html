Can i order over the phone?

You can place your order over the phone by calling our dedicated customer service team at (+84) 0274 222 0372. We're here to assist you with any inquiries or purchases.

I am having difficulty placing an order?

If you're experiencing difficulties placing an order, our support team is here to assist you. Please call us at (+84) 0274 222 0372 or reach out to our online support for immediate help. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we're dedicated to ensuring a smooth shopping experience for you.

What payment methods does accept?

We offer a variety of payment options for your convenience. You can pay using credit or debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and other secure payment methods. If you have any specific preferences or questions, feel free to contact our support team for assistance!

Can i amend my order once placed?

You can amend your order after it has been placed. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible at(+84) 0274 222 0372 or through our online support. We'll do our best to accommodate your request promptly.

How do i know if my order was successful?

After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation email from us containing your order details and a unique tracking number. If you do not receive this email, please check your spam folder or contact our customer service team for assistance. Your order status can also be checked by logging into your account on our website.

What if my order is incorrect?

If you receive an incorrect order, we will return it to stock. Please contact our customer service team immediately at (+84) 0274 222 0372 or through our online support. Provide details of your order and we will resolve the issue as quickly as possible by correcting the order or providing a suitable solution.
